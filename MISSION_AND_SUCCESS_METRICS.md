# MISSION_AND_SUCCESS_METRICS.md (Product Manager)

## Core Mission
To empower Saint Vincent de Paul (Pasco) volunteers by automating the tedious discovery and drafting phases of grant applications, allowing the organization to increase funding volume without increasing human burnout.

## Primary Success Metrics
- **Volume:** 2-3 local grant opportunities scouted per week.
- **Accuracy:** >90% factual accuracy in drafts (Verified by human review).
- **Efficiency:** Reduce manual drafting time for volunteers by 70%.
- **Usability:** 100% "Grandmother Test" pass rate (Binary Approve/Trash decisions).

## Phase Gates
- [x] **Phase 0:** Stakeholder interviews & data ingestion.
- [x] **Phase 1:** Functional Agentic Backend (LangGraph).
- [ ] **Phase 2:** Grandmother UI Frontend (Streamlit/Next.js).
- [ ] **Phase 3:** Production Pilot with 5 historical grants.

## Stakeholders
- SVdP Pasco Volunteers (End Users)
- SVdP Board (Strategic Approval)
- SVdP Finance (Audit/Compliance)
